Welcome to Animal Rescue Foundation

We rescue abandoned animals and help them find loving homes.

There are many ways to volunteer and help animals in need. Volunteering directly at our location is great, but there are other options as well. You can also foster pets in your community, advocate for animals, and help other local shelters and rescue groups in your area.

If you decide to volunteer at our location directly, there are many different opportunities to help. Many of our volunteers love to take walks with our shelter dogs, clean some enclosures to ensure our animals our kept happy and well-cared for, or even just to give an animal some needed company.
